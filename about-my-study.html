Studying

Study is the best inheritence

Because of that proverbe, people are trying hard to teach their children.
Having been well taught and learned a lot is a big target, that can help people to get good quality of life in the future.

There are several steps that students need in order to get the highest grade of education.
Unfortunetely, not everyone can achieve the highest grade as it depends on the supporter, some children don't even go to school as the reasult of poverty.

Every countries have their own way and their own steps of education, so of course Madagascar has its own education too.
So this is about my study which is not that high, let's see!!

About my study

  1. EPP (Ecole Primaire Public)

    I went to EPP school which is in EPP Ankazomianko Vavatenina when I was 7.
    There were 5 steps which i had to pass: 11émé, 10émé, 9émé, 8émé and 7émé. In 7émé there is an exam public, which called CEPE, that every students have to do in Madagascar.

  2. CEG (College d'Enseignement General)

    After the exam, I went to CEG school, that is in CEG Abatobe Vavatenina, in the middle school.
    There were 4 steps and then I did exam public again called BEPC, Which every students have to face.

  3. Lycéé (High school)

    I went to the high school which is in Lycéé Manampisoa Vavatenina when I was 16. I got my Baccalaureat diplomat 2 years after that.

After school

As I said before some parents do not have enough money to afford their children, so the children must leave school not in their purpose.

I left school after my Baccalaureat. I did some business to help my family to survive, hoped not to study at university or somewhere else anymore.

Fortunately, ONJA appeared.

My Study at ONJA

Who is ONJA

ONJA is an Organisation here in Madagascar,
which has a very big mission, that is to propel brilliant young minds in the world’s poorest countries to access equal education and real career opportunities.
It is targeting disadvantaged youth with the most potential, and the really unique part is that these very people will sustain the whole organisation in the future.

ONJA gave me an opportunity to continue my study the 5 years later.

I've been studied english and coding.
Not only about skills but olso about relationship. Students at ONJA have a very good commmunication.
